I was speaking to Gaurav, a friend of mine back in November 2021. During the conversation, we started discussing 
about finances and investing opportunities. He spoke about his plans and the steps he had taken so far. At that 
moment, something clicked in my brain. Like a button. I wanted to invest too! So, I asked him about how to get 
started. I listened with utmost interest. Two months have passed since. Today, I invest monthly and trade a couple 
times per month on the [stock market](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/s/stockmarket.asp). In this post, I will 
explain the steps which help me get started with investing.

## Step 1: Opening an account (*a.k.a.* depot) with a broker

Gaurav mentioned about [Scalable Capital](https://de.scalable.capital/en). It is an online brokerage firm located in 
[Munich](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/M%C3%BCnchen). I researched about the firm and compared it against its competitors  
(like [Trade Republic](https://traderepublic.com/en-de), [Degiro](https://www.degiro.eu/), etc.). In the end, I chose 
Scalable for two main reasons - its automatic tax filing feature and its [pricing options](https://de.scalable.capital/en/trading-costs).

I did the following to open my Scalable Capital depot:

1. I vistited their [website](https://de.scalable.capital/en/register) and followed the instructions. I had to specify an amount which would 
be transferred to my brokerage account after opening. I chose this amount to be &euro;25,00. 

2. Later, I went to a [Deutsche Post](https://www.deutschepost.de/en/home.html) filiale with my passport. I showed the code given to me 
during the previous step and verified my identity.

3. A day later, I got an email from Scalable. I had to share my visa details so that they could open an account with [Baader Bank](https://www.baaderbank.de/).
My brokerage account's securities would be managed by Baader Bank.


A couple of days after I shared my visa information, I received the depot opening and the &25,00 deposit confirmation from Scalable. The entire
process took 5 working days to complete.
